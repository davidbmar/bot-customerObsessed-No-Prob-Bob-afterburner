`★ Insight ─────────────────────────────────────`
**Layered filtering strategy** — The filter now has three tiers: (1) signal-level checks (no-speech prob, audio duration, hallucination patterns) catch STT artifacts, (2) content-level checks (garbage words, low confidence) catch misheard noise, and (3) semantic-level checks (monologue length, repeated phrases, engagement markers) catch valid-sounding speech from wrong sources. Each layer catches what the previous can't.

**N-gram phrase detection** — Using `Counter` on sliding 2-4 word windows is O(n) and catches natural repetition in media (dialogue loops, song lyrics, narration patterns) without false-positiving on normal speech where people occasionally repeat a word.
