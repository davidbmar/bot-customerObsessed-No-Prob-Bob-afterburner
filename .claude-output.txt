`★ Insight ─────────────────────────────────────`
**VAD audio pipeline shortcut**: The push-to-talk path goes MediaRecorder (webm) → FileReader → AudioContext.decodeAudioData → OfflineAudioContext (resample to 16kHz) → PCM int16 → base64. The VAD path skips all of this because `vad-web` already provides a Float32Array at 16kHz in its `onSpeechEnd` callback — we just convert directly to Int16Array and base64-encode. This is significantly simpler and lower latency.

**Echo cancellation simplicity**: Rather than using WebAudio's echo cancellation nodes or complex signal processing, we leverage the fact that VAD has `pause()`/`start()` methods. By listening to the existing `ttsAudioEl` play/ended events, we get echo cancellation with ~4 lines of event wiring.
